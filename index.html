LifeLine - Blood Donation is a real-time free platform to help blood

What is LifeLine - Blood Donation?

LifeLine - Blood Donation is an automated blood service that
connects blood searchers with voluntary donors in a moment
through SMS. LifeLine - Blood Donation is always a free service for all.
