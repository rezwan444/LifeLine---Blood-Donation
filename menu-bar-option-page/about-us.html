About Us

Know about LifeLine - Blood Donation. What, how, and why.

LifeLine - Blood Donation is a web based blood service that connect blood searchers with voluntary blood donors in a moment through website. LifeLine - Blood Donation is a free service for all. LifeLine - Blood Donation started it's journey in 2024.

Why LifeLine - Blood Donation?

Though Bangladesh has more than 160 million people, the number of safe blood bank is very few. Without divisional towns, there is hardly any blood bank. But a huge amount of blood is needed for treatment purposes. A good number of accidents take place every day where blood needs essentially. As a result, people fall in real trouble to manage blood. But there are many blood donors who are interested in donating blood but don’t know who needs blood. The communication gap is resulting in the loss of many lives.

LifeLine - Blood Donation comes into the scenario to reduce or minimize the communication gap and connects people in a moment using the amazing power of Website and email. As LifeLine - Blood Donation services can also be availed by Gmail, people from any class of the society can easily avail of LifeLine - Blood Donation services.

Vision

Ensuring no more death just for the need of blood

Mission

Connecting blood searchers with voluntary blood donors in a moment with the use of technology

Objectives

  • Encouraging voluntary blood donation
  • Creating awareness about safe blood transfer
  • Enabling people to place blood request via Website, Gmail, or Social Platform
  • Connecting voluntary blood donors via Website or email
